GLASSBOX COLLECTIVE AT CARNEGIE HALL

The Glassbox Collective is a group of collaborative artists based in NYC producing new, intermedia work.

I was asked to create the marketing poster for their multidisciplinary exploration of Shakespeare’s Richard II at Carnegie Hall.

The poster I made leaned into an experimental and ominous visual style, using a xerox machine to stretch the typography.
