PROJECT DETAILS

IT HURTS ME - KINETIC TYPE

This project was an assignment given by Stefan Sagmeister during my time as a MFAD student at the School of Visual Arts.

He asked us to choose a phrase that meant something to us and then design something around that phrase. I chose, ‘it hurts me to be unkind'.
